About Us

Chayim International Group is a premier property development firm dedicated to constructing high-quality residential and commercial properties both locally and internationally. With a strategic focus on a fully financed/mortgage-based model, we empower individuals and businesses to own properties without the traditional financial burdens of upfront costs. Our innovative approach ensures accessibility, affordability, and sustainability in housing development. We partner with individuals, families, and businesses to provide exceptional property solutions that turn dreams into reality. Our expertise spans across residential and commercial developments, as well as mortgage financing, and we leverage cutting-edge technology and sustainable building practices to deliver outstanding results.

Our Services

Chayim International Group offers a wide range of property development and financing services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Whether you are looking to invest in residential or commercial properties or seeking accessible financing options, our comprehensive services ensure top-tier, customized solutions for all property requirements.

1. Residential Housing Development

We specialize in creating residential communities that range from affordable housing to luxury living, meeting the needs of all demographics with a variety of housing solutions.

  • Affordable Housing Projects: We are committed to providing affordable housing to individuals and families in need. Our developments feature well-designed, cost-effective homes that do not compromise on quality—creating first-time homeowner opportunities and stimulating local economies.
  • Luxury Apartments and Gated Communities: For those seeking premium living spaces, we offer luxury apartments and gated communities with top-tier amenities such as swimming pools, gyms, and rooftop gardens.
  • Fully Financed Homeownership Plans: Our flagship offering enables individuals to own homes without a large down payment through flexible financing models, low-interest rates, and extended repayment periods.
  • Rent-to-Own Model (Tenant Purchase Model): We provide a unique option where tenants use their rent payments to build toward homeownership by matching a portion of their rent to the mortgage repayment.

2. Commercial Property Development

We develop commercial properties that foster business growth and enhance customer experiences.

  • Office Buildings: Modern office spaces designed to promote productivity and innovation with energy-efficient systems and flexible layouts.
  • Retail Centers and Malls: Innovative retail spaces, including malls and plazas, that enhance the shopping experience with a mix of retail outlets, cafes, and entertainment.
  • Mixed-Use Developments: Integrated developments that combine residential, commercial, and leisure spaces, creating sustainable, walkable communities.

3. International Projects

With a global outlook, we expand our operations into international markets and bring our expertise in property development and financing to new regions.

  • Expansion into Global Markets: Identifying high-growth areas and collaborating with local authorities to provide sustainable housing solutions in regions such as Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.
  • Strategic Partnerships with International Mortgage Financiers: Partnering with international financial institutions to make mortgage financing and property ownership accessible globally.
  • Compliance with Global Building Standards: Adhering to local building codes and international best practices, ensuring sustainability and safety in all our developments.

4. Mortgage & Financing Solutions

In partnership with leading financial institutions, we offer innovative mortgage and financing options for both residential and commercial projects.

  • Residential Financing:
    • Up to 100% Financing
    • Repayment of up to 25 years
    • Interest rates starting as low as 9.5%
    • Buy and Build (BnB): Purchase land and finance the construction of your dream home, even if you don’t yet own land.
  • Commercial Financing:
    • Repayment by Rent Sharing (70%/30% Model)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What services does Chayim International Group offer?

Chayim provides comprehensive property development and financing services, including residential housing (affordable & luxury), commercial developments (offices, malls, mixed-use), international real estate projects, and mortgage/financing solutions.

2. What is the “Buy and Build (BnB)” program?

Buy and Build (BnB) is a financing model that allows clients to purchase land and build a home with up to 100% financing. It removes the burden of upfront capital and enables long-term repayment of up to 25 years at interest rates starting from 9.5%.

3. Do I need a deposit to buy property through Chayim?

No large down payment is required for fully financed plans. Chayim offers flexible financing models designed to make homeownership affordable and accessible.

4. What is the Rent-to-Own model?

This model allows tenants to transition into homeownership by applying their rent payments toward mortgage repayments. It’s ideal for those who may not qualify for traditional financing right away.

5. Are your properties eco-friendly?

Yes. Chayim integrates green technologies like solar panels, rainwater harvesting, energy-efficient materials, and smart home systems into all developments to promote sustainability and reduce utility costs.

6. What countries does Chayim operate in?

Besides Kenya, Chayim has projects in Europe, Africa, and the Middle East, with global partnerships for mortgage financing and international standard compliance.

7. Can businesses also access financing through Chayim?

Yes. Commercial clients can access financing through rent-sharing models and tailored commercial investment options.

8. What is Chayim Kitchen Gardens?

It’s an urban farming initiative integrated into housing projects. It allows residents to grow organic produce using smart technology, promoting food security and healthy living.

9. What makes Chayim different from other developers?

Chayim stands out through 100% financing options, sustainable & smart property designs, community-focused projects, and strong global and financial partnerships.
