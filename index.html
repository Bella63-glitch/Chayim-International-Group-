About Us

Chayim International Group is a premier property development firm dedicated to constructing high-quality residential and commercial properties both locally and internationally. With a strategic focus on a fully financed/mortgage-based model, we empower individuals and businesses to own properties without the traditional financial burdens of upfront costs.

We partner with individuals, families, and businesses to provide exceptional property solutions that turn dreams into reality. Our expertise spans across residential and commercial developments, as well as mortgage financing, and we leverage cutting-edge technology and sustainable building practices to deliver outstanding results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What financing options do you offer?

We offer fully financed, mortgage-based models including up to 100% financing with flexible terms of up to 25 years and competitive interest rates starting as low as 9.5%.

How does the Buy and Build (BnB) model work?

With our Buy and Build (BnB) model, you can purchase a plot in your dream location and build your custom-designed home, all under a single, fully financed plan that eliminates the need for large upfront payments.

How do you incorporate sustainability in your projects?

Our developments integrate green technologies such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting, energy-efficient designs, and smart sensors to ensure lower long-term costs and a reduced environmental footprint.
