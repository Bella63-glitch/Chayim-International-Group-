Frequently Asked Questions

What financing options do you offer?

We offer fully financed, mortgage-based models including up to 100% financing with flexible terms of up to 25 years and competitive interest rates starting as low as 9.5%.

How does the Buy and Build (BnB) model work?

With our Buy and Build (BnB) model, you can purchase a plot in your dream location and build your custom-designed home, all under a single, fully financed plan that eliminates large upfront payments.

How do you incorporate sustainability in your projects?

Our developments integrate green technologies such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting, energy-efficient designs, and smart sensors to ensure lower long-term costs and a reduced environmental footprint.

What green technology initiatives do you implement?

We integrate advanced green solutions including urban farming via Chayim Kitchen Gardens, solar power integration, energy-saving building designs, and rainwater harvesting systems that promote sustainability.

Who are your strategic partners?

We partner with key industry leaders such as OPDA Investment from Portugal and various financial institutions to deliver innovative, eco-friendly, and fully financed property solutions.

How long have you been in the property development industry?

Chayim International Group boasts over 15 years of experience in the property development and finance sectors.

What markets do you serve?

Our operations span local and international markets, developing projects across Kenya and beyond, adapting to diverse customer needs globally.
