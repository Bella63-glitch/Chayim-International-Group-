Benefits of Buy and Build (BnB)

  • Flexible Financing:
    You receive 100% financing, eliminating the need for a hefty down payment.
  • Build on Your Terms:
    Choose a location that aligns with your vision, whether it’s an upscale area or a quieter, more peaceful suburb. Build your home to your exact specifications.
  • Long-Term Financing Options:
    The flexibility of up to 25 years for repayment makes homeownership financially manageable, even with the cost of land and construction combined.
  • Affordable Interest Rates:
    Starting from 9.5%, our interest rates are designed to make the BnB program affordable over the long term.
  • Empowerment:
    The BnB program empowers you to create the home you’ve always wanted, in the location you’ve always dreamed of, without the financial strain of managing multiple loans.
  • Seamless Process:
    From land purchase to construction, Chayim International Group handles every aspect of your project, offering you peace of mind.
